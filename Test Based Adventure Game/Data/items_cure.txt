Hydrogen peroxide 
a chemical compound with the formula H₂O₂. In its pure form, it is a very pale blue liquid, slightly more viscous than water. Used to oxidize wounds.
Magnesium
a cofactor in more than 300 enzyme systems that regulate diverse biochemical reactions in the body, including protein synthesis, muscle and nerve function, blood glucose control, and blood pressure regulation
Iodine 
A mineral found in some foods. The body needs iodine to make thyroid hormones. These hormones control the body's metabolism and many other important functions.
Cortizone 
This medication is used to treat a variety of skin conditions (such as insect bites, poison oak/ivy, eczema, dermatitis, allergies, rash)
White out
Used to redo mistakes with pen or marker on a white surface or white paper.
